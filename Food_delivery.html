Our Special Recipes:

Butter Chicken -

A North Indian classic, Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy dish where tender chicken pieces are marinated in yogurt and spices, then simmered in a velvety tomato-based gravy. It's a delightful balance of tangy and creamy flavors.


Paneer Tikka -

Paneer Tikka is a vegetarian favorite. It consists of marinated cubes of Indian cottage cheese, skewered with vegetables, and grilled or roasted to perfection. The smoky and spicy flavors make it a fantastic appetizer.


Biryani -

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with aromatic spices, long-grain Basmati rice, and either chicken, lamb, or vegetables. Each bite is a delightful blend of spices, saffron, and tender meat or veggies.


Masala Dosa -

A South Indian specialty, Masala Dosa is a crispy, thin rice crepe filled with a spiced potato mixture. Served with coconut chutney and tangy sambar, it's a beloved breakfast dish with a delightful mix of textures and tastes.
